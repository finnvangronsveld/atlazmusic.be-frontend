About

ATLAZ is a hardstyle DJ and producer from Retie, Belgium, on a mission to blend goosebumps and chaos into one unforgettable sound. His style ranges from euphoric and emotional anthems to raw, xtra raw, and uptempo bangers that don't ask for permission - they just hit.

It all started in 2017 when a 12-year-old boy named Finn stumbled across a Headhunterz track and immediately thought, "Whatever that was, I need more." Fast forward a few years, and he learned the art of turning the tables at a local summerbar in 2023. Not long after, he invested in his own gear and hasn't stopped pushing BPMs since.

Inspired by both the beauty and brutality of hardstyle, ATLAZ constantly looks for that perfect mix: melodic builds that pull on the heartstrings followed by drops that punch you in the chest. Whether you're closing your eyes or clenching your jaw - If you're feeling something, he's doing it right.

With a growing arsenal of original tracks and an energy that feels way too big for small-town Belgium, ATLAZ is just getting started. Get in now, before the speakers blow.
